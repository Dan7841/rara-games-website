Tomebound

Lizard Brains. Wizard Problems.

A dual-character roguelite adventure where two cursed lizard wizards fight, puzzle, and platform their way through a shifting, time warped lost city. Play solo or co-op. Survive the curse. Break the loop.

Tomebound is a roguelite dungeon crawler that fuses tactical combat, environmental puzzle-solving, and light platforming, built for both solo and local co-op play.

Story

Bound together by a cursed tome, two rival lizard wizards are trapped in a looping, reality-warped lost city. Each run reshapes the journey into a unique district experience — from combat-heavy Battlegrounds and time-pressured Gauntlets, to puzzle-filled Observatories and treasure-packed Treasuries — ending with a district boss that tests everything you've picked up along the way.

Master both wizards, switch between them in single-player or coordinate in couch co-op. Collect powerful upgrades mid-run, unlock permanent boons between runs, and discover new tactical synergies as you delve deeper into the temple’s mysteries.

With a focus on thoughtful planning, mechanical variety, and character-driven humour, Tomebound offers a fresh, replayable roguelite experience where teamwork is always at the core — even when you’re alone.

Features

  • Run-Based Progression: Procedurally generated paths through four districts, each with unique challenges, traps, and enemies.
  • Dual-Character Core: Swap between wizards in solo play or partner up in couch co-op. Each puzzle, trap, and battle is designed with character synergy in mind.
  • Variety of Room Types:
    • Battlegrounds: Strategic combat arenas packed with enemies.
    • Observatories: Spatial puzzles requiring coordination and timing.
    • Gauntlets: Fast-paced platforming sequences with environmental hazards.
    • Treasuries: Reward-rich side rooms filled with optional challenges.
  • Permanent & Temporary Progression: Gather mid-run power-ups, defeat bosses to unlock permanent upgrades, and build towards your preferred playstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of game is Tomebound?
A roguelite dungeon crawler with puzzle-solving and platforming elements, playable solo (with character switching) or in split-screen co-op.
Do I need two players?
No. Tomebound is fully playable in single-player by switching between the two characters on the fly. But it’s also great with a friend on the couch.
How does progression work?
Each run features procedurally generated room paths, with upgrades earned along the way. Beating bosses unlocks permanent upgrades, letting you build new strategies and survive longer in future loops.
What makes Tomebound stand out?
The dual-character design isn’t just a gimmick — it’s woven into every puzzle, battle, and decision. Whether solo or co-op, you’ll need tactical awareness and clever coordination to win.
What platforms is it coming to?
Tomebound is launching on PC (Steam). Console releases on Switch, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S are under consideration.
What inspired it?
Tomebound draws from the tight combat and progression of Hades, the cooperative mechanics of It Takes Two and Portal 2, and the eerie charm of Cult of the Lamb.
